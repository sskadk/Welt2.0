About Us

Welt Research Pvt. Ltd. is a registered company established for research and experimental development on natural sciences. We are focused on exploration and innovations to make society better while working towards a green and sustainable future.

We aim to solve socio-scientific problems to build a society where no one is left behind— a society achieving “Prosperity and Happiness Together.” Our mission includes the development of technologies for:

  • Pollution and Climate Change
  • Agriculture and Food Security
  • Medical Instrumentation
  • Big Data Management
  • Defence Technologies
  • Other Scientific and Industrial Research
  • Computer Modelling, Simulations, AI & ML, IoT, Software Solutions

These research domains are crucial for addressing today’s global challenges and advancing technologies. From mitigating environmental degradation to strengthening food security, enhancing healthcare with medical instrumentation, managing massive datasets, and innovating in defence and intelligent systems— each area contributes to scientific progress, industrial development, and the overall betterment of society.

Our Team

Shubhansh Tiwari

Founder & President


shubhanshtiwari24@outlook.com

Alumnus of Amity University Haryana in Earth Sciences with a specialization in Climate Studies, he has over 6 years of research experience in Air Quality Dynamics, Dust Storms, Climate Change Impacts, and River Dynamics. His work is published in national and international journals, conference proceedings, and books, including the American Physical Society, and has been featured by NASA. A former student member of the American Physical Society and the Canadian Meteorological and Oceanographic Society, he is currently a member of the Environment and Social Development Association (New Delhi), Institute of Scholars (Bangalore), and Innovative Science Research Society (Shimla). In 2021, he received the “Young Environmentalist Award” from the Environment and Social Development Association at the World Environment Summit. He is also associated with the Centre for Science and Environment and serves on the Governing Council of the Gurukul Global Knowledge Foundation.
